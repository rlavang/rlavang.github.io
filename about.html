About Me

The story so far


My name is Ramin and I'm a GIS specialist who enjoys complex problem solving.  My interest in problem solving has branched into my enthusiasm for programming.  With GIS and programming, I'm able to solve complex problems pertaining to geographic issues.

Much of my GIS experience comes from my bachelor education from the University of Waterloo studying Geomatics with a Computer Science minor.  
I had the pleasure of studying geospatial techniques, and various aspects of technology and programming.

Aside from my education, I had the opportunity to work for several different companies with my internship opportunities gaining valuable experience. Moreover, my personal interest in learning programming and GIS has lead me to create this website and several different projects for fun and learning.

I aim to continue my learning, and working on more complex projects both personal and public.
